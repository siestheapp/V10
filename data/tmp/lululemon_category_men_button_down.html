Casual mens button down shirts that check all the boxes

Essential fits for every seasonour collection of mens button down shirts effortlessly transition from workday to weekend, with fits including:

  • Slim fit: Tailored and snug for a sleek silhouette. Ideal for a fitted look or slim builds.

  • Classic fit: Versatile and comfortable with a balanced looseness. Perfect for everyday wear.

  • Relaxed fit: Spacious and casual for ultimate comfort and freedom of movement. Great for a laid-back vibe

How to choose the right button down shirt

Your button down should be as ready to take on the day as you are. Here's how to choose the perfect one:

  • Fabric technology: Look for materials that offer breathability and sweat wicking to keep you dry and feeling fresh through your 9-5 and beyond.

  • Fit and feel: Soft four-way stretch and wrinkle-free materials are designed for all-day comfort and a straight-out-of-the-dryer look.
