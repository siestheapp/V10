FAQ

What is a "proxi"?
A proxi is a real person who wears the same item in the same size as you. We surface their photos and sizes so you can shop "by proxi" with confidence.
When will Proxi launch?
Public launch is December 2025. Join now to get early access before then.
Does Proxi work for men's clothing?
Not yet. We're starting with women's fashion for early access, with plans to add men's clothing after public launch.
How many items do I need to start?
Just one. Add a single piece you already wear and you'll see proxi matches and recommended sizes on similar items right away. More items = better matches.
